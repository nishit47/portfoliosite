About

I am Nishit Thapa, a 22-year-old filmmaker from Kathmandu, Nepal, currently based in Hattiesburg, MS. A cinema lover since childhood, I love everything film - I love watching, discussing, reading, writing, and making films. Over the years, I have worked as a writer, director, editor, and cinematographer, bringing stories to life from the page to the screen. Personally, I find cinema to be the greatest artistic medium, and I cannot imagine doing anything other than making movies.
